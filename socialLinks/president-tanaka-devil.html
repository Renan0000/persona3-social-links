Persona 3 FES Social Links

President Tanaka (Devil)
Requires LV. 4 Charm and 10000 yen in order to initiate this social link. Tanaka is the only optional social link that will automaticly rank up just by talking to him. He's available on Tuesday and Saturday at night in Pallownia Mall.

Rank 1-10: Automatic

For completing this social link you are able to fuse Beelzebub through Hexagon fusion using Baal Zebul, Abaddon, Lilith, Pazuzu, Succubus and Incubus.
