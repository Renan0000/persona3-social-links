Persona 3 FES Social Links

Nozomi "Gourmet King" (Moon)
This social link requires LV.2 charm and you need to answer questions in this order Pheromone Coffee > Green > Hagakure Bowl, then give him an Odd Morsel from your inventory in order to be started. He's located at Paulownia Mall, Monday-Saturday.

Rank 1: Automatic
Rank 2: Sure, okay
Rank 3: You are the Gourmet King
Rank 4: Oh, are you sick?
Rank 5: Yeah, that's right
Rank 6: Are you feeling sick?
Rank 7: Wiped off the face of ?? > Anything
Rank 8: Oh brother
Rank 9: Anything x2
Rank 10: Automatic

For completing this social link you are able to fuse Sandalphon through Pentagon fusion using Garuda, Horus, Suzaku, Yatagarasu and Gurr.
