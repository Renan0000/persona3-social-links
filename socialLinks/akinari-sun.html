Persona 3 FES Social Links

Akinari (Sun)
This social link will level up Automaticly the whole way through. In order to start this social link you must have LV. 4 Knowledge and the Red Fountain Pen which you get by talking to Korumaru on August 9th or later. The first day this social link is available is on September 6th, afterwards on Sundays at the Shrine.

Rank 1-10: Automatic

For completing this social link you are able to fuse Asura through Hexagon fusion using Vishnu, Suparna, Horus, Jatayu, Quetzalcoatl and Yatagarasu.
