Persona 3 FES Social Links

Nyx Annihilation Team (Judgement)
This is the final social link that will rank up with the story, unless you got the bad ending.

Rank 1: Automatic
Rank 2: Reach floor 215 of Tartarus
Rank 3: Reach floor 220 of Tartarus and defeat Noble Seeker (Resists 4 elements)
Rank 4: Reach floor 224 of Tartarus
Rank 5: Reach floor 228 of Tartarus and defeat Carnal Snake (Resist Fire, Elec and Wind)
Rank 6: Reach floor 236 of Tartarus and defeat World Balance (Resist Physical)
Rank 7: Reach floor 240 of Tartarus
Rank 8: Reach floor 244 of Tartarus and defeat Fierce Cyclops (Blocks Elec, Resists Slash, Strike and Fire)
Rank 9: Reach floor 252 of Tartarus and defeat the Jotun of Grief (Use only pierce melee weapons)
Rank 10: Reach floor 254 of Tartarus

For finishing this social link you are able to fuse Messiah using Thanatos and Orpheus
