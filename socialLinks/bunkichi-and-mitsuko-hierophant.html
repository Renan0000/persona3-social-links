Persona 3 FES Social Links

Bunkichi and Mitsuko (Hierophant)
Requires you to have a leaf from a tree in the yard next to the gym at school. First available on April 25 on Monday-Saturday. They're located at bookworms in Iwatodai Station.

Rank 1: Automatic
Rank 2: Tell him > Thanks > Anything x3
Rank 3: Say "Looking for something?" > I'll help you look > Anything x3
Rank 4: Anything x2 > Now, you have me worried
Rank 5: Say "I'll root out the truth!" > Anything
Rank 6: Say "Please stop quarrelling" > Anything
Rank 7: Ask why she's crying > That's great > Anything
Rank 8: Skips to Rank 9
Rank 9: Anything x2
Rank 10: Automatic

For completing this social link you are able to fuse Kohryu through Cross fusion using Byakko, Suzaku, Seiryuu and Genbu.
